About Me

My name is Jadelyn Drake, and I’m a contemporary artist based out of Seattle Washington. I’m currently a Senior at the University of Washington pursuing a BA in Interdisciplinary Visual Arts (IVA) focusing on the conversation the mind has with the body. Throughout my research, the parallels between mental thought and physicality compared to digital versus traditional art have sparked a chord within me, for my art mostly focuses on deconstructing the human brain into something more digestible. With the use of 2D mediums and Sculpture, my goal is to use art as a vessel for healing, hoping to bring my viewers along for the ride.
